Frequently Asked Questions

If you were wandering...

Is standard car driving licence suitable to drive your campervans?

In most cases with a standard driving licence you may drive a vehicle of maximum total mass of 3.5 ton. Our campervans lie within this limit.

Do I need special insurance?

Our campervans come with standard insurance which is subject to excess fee of £1000 (worst case scenario). You may cover this with extra insurance should you wish to.

I never drove a large vehicle. Is it difficult?

You need to be extra careful, especially in the beginning. Usually it takes a few days to get used to it. Then you will enjoy fantastic visibility a high vehicle usually offers.

What if I break something?

We ask our custommers to take good care of the expensive vehicle and its equipment but we understand sometimes things go other ways than anticipated.

For motor incidents we offer vehicle insurance.

For other kinds of damage we go through thorough check before and after the rent. Then the damage cost is assesed by our Garage Crew according to their parts and labour cost list.

Can I go abroad with your campervan?

We request that the customer lets us know about their intention to travel abroad so we can arrange for extra insurance and other precautions.

How much does it cost?

Fees vary according to times of year, holidays etc. Please contact us for details!

Where should I park at night?

Campsites are a popular choice. There are multiple options varying according to available ammenities, location etc. You may check availability on webpages and apps of big camping clubs like Camping And Caravanning Club™, internet campsite search engines or Google Maps.

You may also try to be even more adventorous and wildcamp. Check legal considerations in the desired area beforehand.

Do I need to take my own kitchen utensils?

We provide some basic utensils simmilar to the ones that could be needed on a camping trip (tin cups, cuttlery, pan). You may take your own equipment should you so desire.
