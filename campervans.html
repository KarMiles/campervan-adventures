Our Campervans

Campervan One

| Fregata

Ford Transit, Extra high roof, Long-wheel base

Attributes

This comfortable campervan has a roof high enough for most people to be able to stand straight inside.

During the day a table may be arased to have your coffee in the morning. At night the sofa spreads into full-size double bed.

Amenities

It has all amenities necessary for cooking your dinner: a two-burner gas stove and a microwave; and for chilling your bottle of Pinot Grigio - silent gas fridge.

It has a fully functioning hot-water shower and a toilet. If the night gets a bit chilly a super-efficient heating is available (2kW electric if on electric hook-up and diesel heater when out and about).

For charging your applieces series of 12V, 240V and USB sockets are available.

Campervan Two

| Festival

Volkswagen T2 Westfalia

Attributes

This classic campervan stole hearts of many men and women who can appreciate beauty and adventure!

During the day a table may be arased to have your coffee in the morning. At night the sofa spreads into a small double bed.

Amenities

It has basic amenities necessary for cooking your dinner: a one-burner gas stove; and for chilling your bottle of Pinot Grigio - a mini 12V cooler.

If the night gets a bit chilly a super-efficient heating is available (2kW diesel heater).

For charging your applieces 12V and USB sockets are available.
