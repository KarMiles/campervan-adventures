Campervan Adventures

Compervan One

Fregata

Ford Transit, Extra high roof, Long-wheel base

Comforts

This comfortable campervan has a roof high enough for most people to be able to stand straight inside.

It has all amenities necessary for cooking your dinner and chilling your bottle of Pinot Grigio.

It has a fully functioning hot-water shower and a toilet. If the night gets a bit chilly a super-efficient heating is available (2kW electric if on hook-up and diesel heater when out and about).
